Standard Drop (Mon - Thu)

The Rule: Monday through Thursday, you can drop your spot freely. If there is a waitlist, the first person waiting will automatically be bumped up to fill your spot.

Are you sure you want to drop?

Late Drop (Friday - Saturday)

The Rule: It is past the Friday 8 AM deadline. To prevent leaving an incomplete court stranded, late drops follow this automated process:

  • If there is a waitlist: We will email the first player waiting. You will be marked as "Pending Sub" and released only once they click accept.
  • If there is NO waitlist: You will be marked "Needs Sub". You are responsible for finding a replacement and entering their info on the dashboard.
Provide a Sub

The Rule: If you already have a confirmed sub to take your place, you can enter their info here at any time. They will immediately take your spot on the roster and receive a confirmation email with court details.

Request a Guest

The Rule: Want to bring a friend to play? Submit their details here. Jim will receive an alert to review. Once approved, your guest will be automatically added to the roster!
