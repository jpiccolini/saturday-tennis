Gang Guidelines

  • Commitment: Commit only if you can be there for the entire time (2+ hours).
  • Guests: If you add a guest, you are their guide and sub-coordinator if they bail.
  • Automatic Waitlist: The first 24 players are confirmed. Anyone after is automatically waitlisted. If a confirmed player cancels, the app promotes the next person and emails them automatically.
  • Cancellation: If you drop out, click "Cancel My Spot" so the next person is notified immediately.
  • Friday Lock-In: Once locked in on Friday, you are responsible for playing or providing a sub.
  • Parking @ Dawson: Drive up main drag, over speed bump, bending right. Turn right onto Henderson Court. Park in the nearest lot on your left (not on dirt/grass).

Keep bringing the spirit, it matters!
