🎾 How It Works
✅ How to Sign Up
  1. Enter your 4-digit code and click Enter.
  2. Once logged in, click the green Sign Me Up! button.
  3. You'll appear on the roster immediately. If all playing spots are filled, you'll be placed on the waitlist automatically.
⚠️ How to Cancel (Before Friday Morning)
  1. Log in and click Cancel My Spot.
  2. You'll be removed immediately and the next waitlisted player moves up.
  3. Please cancel before Friday morning to avoid triggering the sub process.
🆘 How to Request a Sub (Friday or Later)
  1. Log in and click Drop / Need a Sub.
  2. Choose "I need a sub" — this flags your spot and emails the next waitlisted player with a sub offer.
  3. You stay on the roster as NEEDS SUB until someone accepts.
  4. Once a sub accepts, you're removed and they take your spot.
  5. If no one is on the waitlist, your spot will be listed as open — reach out to the group directly.
📬 How to Accept a Sub Offer (Waitlist Players)
  1. If you're on the waitlist, you may receive a sub offer email when a playing spot opens Friday or later.
  2. Log in to the site — you'll see an Accept Spot button at the top of the sidebar.
  3. Click it to confirm. You'll be moved from the waitlist to the playing roster.
  4. If you don't respond in time, the offer passes to the next person on the waitlist.
📬 Not Getting Our Emails? Check Spam!
  • Search your spam / junk folder for "Saturday Tennis Gang" and mark it Not Spam.
  • Gmail users: drag one of our emails from "Promotions" or "Spam" into your Primary tab.
  • Add our sender address to your contacts so future emails land in your inbox automatically.
  • Email changed? Log in, click Profile, and update it there — this keeps you on all future signup and sub-offer emails.
